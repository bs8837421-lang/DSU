about University

The Dhanalakshmi Srinivasan University (DSU) has been established under the Tamil Nadu Private Universities Act, 2019, located in Tiruchirappali, TamilNadu, India. Uniqueness of DSU lies in its multi-disciplinary nature in offering a wide range of academic programmes encompassing medicine, paramedical, engineering and agricultural sciences. Our motto is “education for the real world” with dedication and commitment towards nurturing the future generation. Green ambience with state-of-the-art infrastructure along with top-class faculty aims to serve the need of national and international students.
